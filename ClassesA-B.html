Class A


Class A, the highest-ranked class at the Tokyo Metropolitan Advanced Nurturing High School, is a symbol of excellence and privilege, embodying the school’s ideal of fostering Japan’s future leaders. Under the strategic and manipulative leadership of Arisu Sakayanagi, Class A consistently maintains its dominant position by excelling in academics, special exams, and social manipulation. Sakayanagi’s leadership is the cornerstone of their success; despite her physical disability, she commands the class with unparalleled intelligence and charisma, ensuring unity and loyalty among her peers. Her ability to anticipate and counter the moves of rival classes, combined with her talent for psychological warfare, makes Class A a formidable opponent. With their significant lead in class points and superior access to resources, they enjoy luxurious privileges and a level of security unmatched by the other classes. However, their success is not without challenges, as overconfidence and occasional internal power struggles can leave them vulnerable to the relentless strategies of lower-ranked classes like Class D. Despite this, Class A’s cohesion and Sakayanagi’s tactical brilliance ensure their continued dominance, making them a central force in the competitive environment of the school.

Sakayanagi Arisu

Sakayanagi Arisu, the leader of Class 1-A (later 2-A), is a master strategist and one of the most intelligent students at the Advanced Nurturing High School. Despite her physical disability, which requires her to use a cane, Arisu commands respect and fear through her sharp intellect and manipulative abilities. She is an expert at psychological warfare, often using her words and actions to dismantle her opponents without lifting a finger. Her calm and composed demeanor makes her a formidable presence, and she rarely shows any signs of weakness. Arisu's leadership style is one of calculated precision. She views her classmates as pieces on a chessboard, moving them strategically to secure victory. Her ability to read people and predict their actions allows her to stay several steps ahead of her rivals. However, Arisu is not without compassion. She genuinely cares for her class's success and is willing to make sacrifices to ensure they remain at the top. Arisu’s rivalry with Ayanokōji is one of mutual respect, as both recognize the other's brilliance. Unlike Ryūen, who relies on force, Arisu uses intellect and persuasion to achieve her goals. This makes her a stark contrast to other leaders and highlights her unique approach to competition. Despite her confidence, Arisu faces challenges, including the need to balance her personal ambitions with her responsibilities as a leader. Her journey is one of overcoming limitations, both physical and emotional, to maintain her position as one of the school's top leaders. Arisu’s intelligence, charisma, and determination make her a standout character in the series, embodying the idea that true strength comes from the mind.

Class B


Class B is often seen as the most balanced and cohesive class at the Tokyo Metropolitan Advanced Nurturing High School, largely due to the leadership of Honami Ichinose. Known for her warm and charismatic personality, Ichinose fosters unity and optimism within her class, creating an atmosphere of trust and mutual support. This sense of teamwork allows Class B to perform consistently well, maintaining their position near the top of the rankings. Unlike Class A’s cutthroat tactics, Ichinose relies on her classmates’ strengths and her reputation as a fair and trustworthy leader. However, their idealistic approach can sometimes make them vulnerable to the more aggressive strategies of Class A or the cunning manipulations of other classes. Despite these challenges, Class B remains resilient, with Ichinose’s unwavering resolve and ability to inspire her peers keeping them competitive. Their strength lies in their unity and their willingness to work together, making them a formidable force in the school’s high-stakes environment.

Ichinose Honami

Ichonose Honami, the leader of Class 1-B (later 2-B), is known for her warm and approachable demeanor, which contrasts starkly with the competitive nature of the school. She is a charismatic figure, loved by her classmates and respected by students from other classes. Honami’s natural kindness and optimism make her a unifying force in her class, enabling her to lead effectively without resorting to manipulation or coercion. However, her seemingly perfect personality hides deeper insecurities and burdens. Honami is a master at maintaining her image, using her charm and wit to navigate challenges and defuse conflicts. Her ability to appear flawless often makes her a target of envy and suspicion, particularly from students who see her as too good to be true. Beneath her cheerful exterior, Honami harbors secrets that she is determined to keep hidden, fearing they may tarnish her reputation and weaken her leadership. As a leader, Honami balances empathy with decisiveness, making her an effective strategist. She excels in academics and sports, ensuring her class remains competitive in school events. Her relationships with other leaders, such as Horikita and Ryūen, highlight her ability to negotiate and collaborate while protecting her class's interests. Despite her strengths, Honami is not immune to stress and self-doubt, which occasionally threaten to undermine her composure. Honami’s layered personality and leadership skills make her one of the most intriguing characters in the series. Her journey is one of maintaining her image while confronting her vulnerabilities, all while striving to lead her class to success.
