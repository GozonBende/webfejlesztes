About the Series

Classroom of the Elite is a popular Japanese light novel series written by Shōgo Kinugasa and illustrated by Shunsaku Tomose. It began serialization in 2015 through Media Factory’s MF Bunko J imprint. The series quickly gained a large following due to its unique exploration of meritocracy and psychological warfare in a fictional high school setting, the Tokyo Metropolitan Advanced Nurturing School, which trains Japan's future elites. Story and Themes The narrative follows Kiyotaka Ayanokōji, a highly intelligent but reserved student in Class-D, a section for students deemed “inferior.” The school operates on a strict meritocratic system where students can earn or lose privileges based on their academic and social performance. This intense, competitive environment brings out manipulative, ambitious, and resilient characteristics in students, creating a suspenseful and psychologically rich storyline. Light Novels and Sales The series is divided into two major arcs: the original Classroom of the Elite arc (2015-2019) and the ongoing Year 2 arc, which started in 2020. The novels have been a commercial success, especially among young adults, selling millions of copies in Japan. The Year 2 arc is set to conclude in November 2024 with Volume 12.5. Internationally, the novels are published in English by Seven Seas Entertainment, extending their reach to a global audience and driving up sales and popularity in Western markets.

At first glance he is just a tottaly avreage boy, his grades are not good but not bad either, his pyshical appearance is not outstanding, he is handsome but not too handsome. A tottaly normal boy in Class D. At least thats what we would tell about him after reading the first few volumes. However thats only the surface of hus character, this is his camoflage. He is a calculating, extremely inteligent, physicaly gifted individual. He can manipulate people and classes as easily as he wakes up. He can expell anyone and can win anything he wants. However due his personality and character ,he tries to lay back and stay hidden. As we progress in the series we can get a gasp of his abilities. He is manipulative, smart, extremely neutral, ruthless and purposeful. His one and only goal is to improve himself and win at any cost, he would sacrifie anything and anyone if that would be nessecery for the sake of victory. The briliancy of his character is that even tho you now that he is not a good person at all, you will enjoy every single minutes of reading his actions, hisbunique thinking and background is interesting, he is suprisingly funny , but can be clumsy at times. He is simply an extremly well written character who earned the fans respect and love.
